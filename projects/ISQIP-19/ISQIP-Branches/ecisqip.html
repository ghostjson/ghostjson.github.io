    Summer School

    ISQIP is a week-long internship training program offering both technical and managerial sessions, for the overall development of the student during the summer vacation.

    Industrial Visit

    Industrial Visits sensitize students to the practical challenges that organizations face in the technical world. It provides you an opportunity to learn through interaction, working methods and employment practices ,thereby giving a clarity on various technical concepts. Visiting a company gives you a practical perspective on the world of work.

    Internship

    ISQIP is aimed to aid the students to envisage an industrial approach towards their field of study by paving them a way to get their hands on internship opportunities by leading startups and firms in Kerala.
